Information

How do I add a new item or a new category?
Tap on the add button in the upper left corner in kitchen page.

How do I delete an item?
Tap on the grey X next to the item name. Tap again to successfully delete.

How do I move items or categorize them?
Drag and drop is enabled so you can move and reorder items around. To categorize, drag the item under the category of your choice.
How do I add a new item to the shopping list?
Tap on the add button in the upper left corner in shopping page.

How do I export items?
Open the shopping cart, and click on the kitchen icon next to the item. Note: This will only export one item at a time. To export all items, click on export all at the bottom.

How do I add items from shopping list to shopping cart?
Drag the desired items from shopping list, and drop on top of the cart icon.
How do I add a new recipe?
Tap on the plus icon in the top left corner of the recipe page.

How do I view a recipe?
Tap on the recipe name you would like to view. A popup window will appear with more information about the recipe.

How do i delete all recipes?
How do I change user name?
Under User Name in Settings page, enter the new username of your choice and tap on change.

How do I change current kitchen?
Under Your Kitchens in Settings page, you will see a list of kitchens you have joined. Choose the kitchen of your choice, and tap on the green check next to the name to set it as current kitchen.

How do I delete or leave a kitchen?
Under Your Kitchens in Settings page, choose the kitchen from the list that you would like to leave. Tap on the red X next to the name to delete the kitchen.

How to add a new kitchen?
To create a new one, type in a kitchen name of your choice in a textbox above the list of kitchens, and click create.

How to accept or decline invitations?
Tap on the bell icon in the top right corner of settings page. Tap on the green check to accept or red X to decline.
How do I send messages?
Go to the info page, and on the top right corner, tap on the message icon.

How do I add units??
Under Measurement Units in settings page, you will see a list of units already created. To create a new one, add a unit of your choice in the textbox, and click on create.
